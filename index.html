We are the Sparkans FIRST Robotics Team, a group of passionate and dedicated high school students from STEM Innovation Academy in Calgary, Alberta. STEM Innovation Academy opened its doors to Grade 10 students in Fall 2022, and has now welcomed over 300 new students in Fall 2023. The newly founded Sparkans have shown commitment to this team and we are excited to continue our journey in the FIRST Robotics Competition. We are proud to be building the foundation for future generations of Sparkans and of STEM IA High School students!
